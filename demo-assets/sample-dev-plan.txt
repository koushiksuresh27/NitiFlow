LOCAL AREA DEVELOPMENT PLAN 2026 - BENGALURU NORTH
--------------------------------------------------

1. Government High School Modernization
Location: Kempegowda Ward
Category: schools
Estimated Budget: ₹ 20,00,000
Details: Complete overhaul of the existing primary school including 4 new smart classrooms and a science laboratory.

2. Primary Health Centre Extension
Location: Chowdeshwari Ward
Category: health
Estimated Budget: ₹ 35,00,000
Details: Construction of a new maternity wing and upgrading current hospital beds with modern equipment.

3. Attur Arterial Road Relay
Location: Attur
Category: roads
Estimated Budget: ₹ 50,00,000
Details: Relaying 3 kilometers of the main arterial road including proper storm water drainage on both sides.

4. Comprehensive Drainage Overhaul
Location: Jakkur
Category: sanitation
Estimated Budget: ₹ 85,00,000
Details: Extending the underground drainage system to newly developed layouts to prevent monsoon flooding.

5. LED Street Lighting Implementation
Location: Thanisandra
Category: street_lights
Estimated Budget: ₹ 12,00,000
Details: Installation of 200 high-efficiency LED street lights across major intersections for better night safety.

6. Youth Vocational Skill Centre
Location: Vidyaranyapura
Category: skills
Estimated Budget: ₹ 45,00,000
Details: Building a state-of-the-art vocational training centre focused on electronics, IT skills, and tailoring.
